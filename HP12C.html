Important things that you need to know about financial calculators are:
  • RPN (Reversal Polish Notation) enhaces performace
  • When making calculations, the keystroke has to be: number and then signal. For example, to find out the result of 7 plus 5, you have to type 7 ENTER 5 +
  • F and G keys are very important. When you press the F key, you are activating the orange functions that are above the keys. When you press the G key, you are activating the blue functions that are below the keys
  • There are some very important financial keys, that you should notice: n means number of periods, i means interest rates, PV means present values, PMT means payment and FV means future value
