Financial Planning
 

Do you keep waiting for payday just to complain about how your money desapeared super fast? Maybe it is time for you to start planning your finances!!

Start mapping your earnigs and expenses. You can find a model below:

Look how cool  

If you don't have all the information, it is ok! Just fill with what you got right now and you can improve it just by taking 15 minutes to fill it every day.

The next step is understanding where you are spending too much money. Sometimes we are just surprised as how much we spend in a certain category. Be sure to cut unecessary expenses if you want to have extra money to invest.
